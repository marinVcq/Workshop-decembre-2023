Memory Game Rules

Welcome to the Memory Game!

You will be presented with a list of 10 words. Your task is to memorize the words in the given order.

After clicking "Start," you will have 10 seconds to memorize the words.

Once the memorization time is up, you'll be asked to enter the words in the correct order.

Good luck and have fun!
